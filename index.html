About This Visualization

Based on "Reimagining Grid Flexibility in a Constrained Power System" by Keith Katyora & Komla Folly (University of Cape Town), SAUPEC 2026.

The objective is to help users understand how declining coal performance, renewable integration, transmission constraints, and storage needs interact to shape South Africa's power system.
