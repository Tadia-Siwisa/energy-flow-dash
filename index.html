About This Visualization

This visualization is a systems map inspired by research into South Africa's energy transition.

Its purpose is to help users understand the relationships between generation, transmission, storage, and demand.

The dashboard is not intended to identify operational bottlenecks or provide real-time grid analysis.

Some infrastructure locations are based on public information, while certain flow representations remain conceptual.
